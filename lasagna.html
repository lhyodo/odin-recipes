Lasagna Recipe

This delicious lasagna recipe comes from Southern Texas, and is a winner of the recent 2022 Houston Lasagna Festival. Made by Erika Thomas, this legendary recipe comes from her late grandmother Susan Powell, a globally renowned and award winning chef. The recipe leaves you with a stringy, mouthwatering cheesy layer, under a hearty and filling meat center.

Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, diced
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 1/2 tea spoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 jars(about 24 ounces each) spaghetti juice
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 packages (8 ounces each) no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces (about 2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese
