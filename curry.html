Japanese Curry Recipe

This creamy and flavorful Japanese curry recipe is made with a Golden Curry base, and thin layers of beef chock.

Ingredients
  • Golden Curry base, 2 boxes
  • 5 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 2 lbs beef chuck shabu shabu
Steps
  1. Cut carrots, onions, and potatoes into appropriate sizes and stir fry on medium heat in a pan for 10 minutes.
  2. Boil shabu shabu beef.
  3. Fill 2400ml of water in a pot and bring to a boil, for the curry.
  4. Cut curry base into one inch square pieces.
  5. Place carrots, onions, and potatoes in boiling water, along with the curry base. Cook for 10 minutes while mixing well.
  6. Put the boiled shabu shabu beef into the curry. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  7. Turn off heat, and wait at least 30 minutes before serving.
